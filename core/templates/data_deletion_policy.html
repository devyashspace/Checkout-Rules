Data Deletion Policy

Data Deletion Policy for Oeme Checkout Rules

Oeme Checkout Rules is developed and maintained by Oeme Methods. We respect merchant privacy and provide a clear process for requesting the deletion of store data associated with the App.


Automatic Data Deletion

When a merchant uninstalls Oeme Checkout Rules from their Shopify store, the App automatically begins the process of removing stored data associated with that store. This includes configuration data, rule settings, and any store-related information used to operate the App.

All applicable store data is deleted within a reasonable period after the App is uninstalled unless retention is required for legal or security purposes.


Manual Data Deletion Request

If a merchant wishes to request the deletion of their data manually, they may submit a request by contacting our support team.

To request data deletion, please email us at: [support-email@example.com]

Please include the following details in your request:

- Shopify store domain (e.g., your-store.myshopify.com)

- Contact email associated with the store

- A request stating that you want your data deleted

Once we receive the request, we will verify the store ownership and process the deletion of the requested data within a reasonable timeframe.


Contact Information

If you have questions regarding this Data Deletion Policy, please contact us at: [support-email@example.com]
