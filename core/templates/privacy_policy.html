Privacy Policy for Oeme Checkout Rules

Oeme Checkout Rules ("the App") provides merchants with the ability to create rules that hide or control payment and shipping methods during checkout. This Privacy Policy describes how personal information is collected, used, and shared when you install or use the App in connection with your Shopify-supported store.


Information We Collect

When you install the App, we are automatically able to access certain types of information from your Shopify account. This may include store information such as the store name, store email address, store domain, and other data required for the App to function properly.

The App may also access order-related or checkout-related information that is necessary to apply the rules configured by the merchant, such as shipping methods, payment methods, cart details, and checkout attributes.

We do not collect or store sensitive customer payment information such as credit card numbers.


How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely to provide and improve the functionality of the App. Specifically, the data is used to apply merchant-configured rules that control which payment or shipping methods are shown during checkout.

We may also use the information to maintain the performance of the App, troubleshoot issues, and improve our services.


Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We only share information when necessary to operate the App or when required by law.

In some cases, information may be processed by secure service providers that help us operate and maintain the App infrastructure.


Data Retention

We retain store information only for as long as it is necessary to provide the App’s services. If you uninstall the App, we will delete or anonymize your store data within a reasonable period unless we are legally required to retain it.


Your Rights

Merchants have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of their data held by the App. If you would like to make such a request, please contact us using the information provided below.


Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes to our practices, operational requirements, or legal obligations.
